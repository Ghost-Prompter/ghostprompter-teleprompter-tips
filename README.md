## 💡 Essential Laptop Teleprompter Tips

* **Positioning for Perfect Eye Contact:** Place your scrolling text container directly below your laptop’s webcam lens. This minimizes eye movement and makes it look completely natural to your audience.
* **Optimizing Background Opacity:** Adjust the overlay transparency based on your desktop environment. A 30% to 40% text background opacity usually offers the best balance between script readability and desktop visibility.
* **Pacing with Hotkeys:** Don't let a fixed scroll speed rush your delivery. Map your keyboard shortcuts to easily pause and resume the script, allowing for natural pauses and unscripted ad-libs.
