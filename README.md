## 🚀 Why Use an Invisible Teleprompter?

Traditional setups require bulky external hardware or distracting secondary screens that break your eye contact with the camera. GhostPrompter solves this by operating as a fully customizable, **invisible teleprompter** overlay.

* 🧑‍💻 **Type and Click Seamlessly:** Read your script directly over your active application windows while you code, design, or navigate live software.
* 🔒 **Hidden from Screen Shares:** While you can see your text scrolling perfectly, the overlay remains completely invisible to your audience on Zoom, Microsoft Teams, and OBS Studio.
* 🎛️ **Total Desktop Control:** Use global hotkeys to adjust scroll speed, pause, or rewind your script without ever clicking away from your presentation workflow.

## 💡 Essential Laptop Teleprompter Tips

1. **Positioning for Perfect Eye Contact:** Place your scrolling text container directly below your laptop’s webcam lens. This minimizes eye movement and makes it look completely natural to your audience.
2. **Optimizing Background Opacity:** Adjust the overlay transparency based on your desktop environment. A 30% to 40% text background opacity usually offers the best balance between script readability and desktop visibility.
3. **Pacing with Hotkeys:** Don't let a fixed scroll speed rush your delivery. Map your keyboard shortcuts to easily pause and resume the script, allowing for natural pauses and unscripted ad-libs.
